## 💡 Technical Decisions

### Why Vanilla JavaScript?
- ✅ No build process required
- ✅ Faster load times
- ✅ Easier to understand and modify
- ✅ No dependency management
- ✅ Works offline

### Why No Framework?
- ✅ Simpler deployment
- ✅ Better performance
- ✅ Smaller bundle size
- ✅ More portable
- ✅ Easier to showcase

### Why CSS Variables?
- ✅ Easy theming
- ✅ Better maintainability
- ✅ Dynamic updates possible
- ✅ Good browser support
